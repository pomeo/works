HOTEL GREIF

BOLZANO, ITALY

"Greif" is the German word for "Griffin", a mythical creature with the wings, beak and talons of an eagle, and the body of a lion. Just as it combines strength and wisdom, temporal powers with supernatural, so Hotel Greif compounds tradition with modernity, and architectural substance with creative energy. The main entrance is a clear indication of the hotel's approach: a transparent steel and glass construction, framed by the antique wood of the original portal.
