LACE MARKET HOTEL

BOLZANO, ITALY

Lace Market Hotel is Nottingham's first boutique hotel. A conversion of 4 Georgian and Victorian townhouses, the hotel is a hive of activity in what has been described as Nottingham's "up and coming" city center district. Original elements, such as the sash windows, architraves and coving have been restored and contrasted with modern, custom designed furniture, brushed steel finishes and natural colors. >>>
